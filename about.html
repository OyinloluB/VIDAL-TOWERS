ABOUT US

Vidaltowers...

Vidaltowers is a privately owned business consulting firm, offering specialist advisory services to B2B and B2C industry. Vidaltowers Limited is a key player in business consulting and advisory services in Nigeria and Africa region, bridging gaps between the management approaches and its actual implementation plans. The company is recognized as one of the most trusted advisors to many business leaders, governments, and institutions in the field of management consulting.
We help our clients make distinctive, lasting and significant improvements to the perfomance of their organizations. We tackle their most difficult issues and serious challenges. Adding a real value when it comes to overcoming the pain areas and bridging the gaps between the business objectives and their current status.

Why Vidaltowers?

We believe that our clients' success is our success; we believe in professionalism and teamwork. We consistently maintain high standards for service and consultants to be always able to bring the best team of senior consultants to bear on every single project. We understand our clients business needs, we comprehend their business specific language. We live the way they live. We help enterprises explore extraordinary opportunities, manage and sustain growth and maximum revenue.
Vidaltowers has succeeded in developing frameworks, models and tools that serve to identify our clients' specific requirements and cater for addressing them in the most effective and efficient manner. However, we are presently working towards continuous improvement and stretching our potential.

Our Vision

To become world leader in consultancy so as to be number one in terms of quality and clients satisfaction with advanced analytical solutions.

Our Mission

We are committed to satisfaction of our clients by providing them with uncompromising quality, superior level of consulting, services of exceptional value through continuous innovation approaches.
